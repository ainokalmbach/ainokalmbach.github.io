Research Teaching CV

I am a Senior Researcher at Etla Economic Research. My research focuses on the economics of education and the economic effects of interactions between immigrants and natives. I received my PhD in Economics from Aalto University School of Business in 2024.

Research interests: Economics of education, labor economics
